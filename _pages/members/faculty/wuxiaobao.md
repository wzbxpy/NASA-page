Dr. Xiaobao Wu is an assistant professor and PhD advisor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. He received his PhD from Nanyang Technological University in Singapore, and his master’s and bachelor’s degrees from Tsinghua University and Southeast University, respectively. He has previously been a visiting scholar at the University of California, Santa Barbara, and a postdoctoral researcher at Nanyang Technological University. His primary research interests include artificial intelligence, natural language processing, large language models, and related areas. He has published more than 40 papers in top international conferences and journals such as NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, ACL, and EMNLP. He has long served as an area chair for conferences including ACL and EMNLP, and he received the ACL 2025 Area Chair Award.
